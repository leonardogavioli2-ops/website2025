Tournament Information

The tournament will be on the 19th of November. It will be held in the computer science room. We will play Mario Kart 8 Deluxe on a Switch 2. The event will begin with two preliminary rounds to determine who advances. Both rounds will be in the Banana Grand Prix, and the highest scorer from each team will move on to the finals. The finalists—Charles, Mr. Daniels, Mr. Chambers, and Leonardo—will each represent a team: Yellow, Green, Red, and Blue. The finalists will compete to determine the best racer, and the team with the most points at the end of the Grand Prix will win the cup.
